Mill Monitoring System

A reliability engineering / Green Belt project focused on improving preventative maintenance completion through better line-status visibility and maintenance communication.

Project overview

This project came from reliability engineering work in a manufacturing environment. The goal was to support preventative maintenance completion by making equipment status and high-priority work easier for maintenance to see and act on.

Problem

Preventative maintenance is difficult to complete consistently when maintenance teams do not have clear, immediate visibility into line status, equipment availability, and high-priority work.

  • Maintenance needed better visibility of when lines were available.
  • PM opportunities could be missed when information was not immediate.
  • The issue was partly technical and partly a systems/communication problem.

Approach

The monitoring system was intended to communicate useful line-status and work information in a simple, visible way. The goal was not to create a complicated system. The goal was to surface the right information at the right time so maintenance could respond.

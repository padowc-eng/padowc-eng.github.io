Overview

ATLAS is a passive knee exoskeleton concept for reducing knee strain during hiking. The design uses mechanical assistance instead of motors or batteries.

My role

  • Led mechanical concept development and prototype direction.
  • Worked through mechanism layout, CAD decisions, and design tradeoffs.
  • Balanced weight, comfort, cost, usability, and manufacturability.

Engineering work

Mechanism

Passive assist during knee motion. The design needed useful energy return without fighting the user.

Prototype

Focused on fit, adjustment, durability, and whether the device could be practical outdoors.

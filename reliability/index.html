Overview

This page covers reliability engineering work in a manufacturing environment: recurring equipment issues, downtime reduction, preventative maintenance support, and practical systems improvement.

Core problem

Preventative maintenance is harder to complete when maintenance teams do not have clear visibility into line status, equipment availability, and high-priority work. The issue was not only technical. It was also a communication and systems problem.

  • Maintenance needed better visibility of line status.
  • PM work needed to be easier to act on when equipment became available.
  • Information needed to be immediate and practical, not buried in disconnected systems.

Green Belt project

My Lean Six Sigma Green Belt project focused on improving PM completion through better visibility of equipment status and high-priority maintenance work. 

// project logic
problem: "PM completion was inconsistent"
cause: "maintenance lacked immediate visibility into line status / work priority"
approach: "surface useful status and work information more clearly"
goal: "make PM opportunities easier to recognize and act on"

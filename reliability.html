Reliability Engineering

Overview

This page covers my reliability engineering work in a manufacturing environment.

  • Supported preventative maintenance improvement.

Core Problem

Preventative maintenance is difficult to complete consistently when maintenance teams do not have clear visibility into line status or high-priority work.

  • The project focused on improving maintenance communication.
  • The goal was to make equipment availability easier to understand.
  • Better visibility helped maintenance know when to act.

Green Belt Project

My Lean Six Sigma Green Belt work focused on improving PM completion through better line-status visibility.

  • Developed a mill monitoring concept.
  • Displayed line status and high-priority work information.
  • Connected communication problems to maintenance performance.

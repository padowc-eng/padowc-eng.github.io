Design overview

This page is meant for roles that care about mechanical design and CAD ability. It should eventually show CAD screenshots, assemblies, drawings, mechanisms, prototype parts, and the reasoning behind design choices.

What belongs here

  • SolidWorks assemblies and part models.
  • Exploded views and section views.
  • Mechanism screenshots showing how parts move or interface.
  • Prototype parts and design iterations.
  • FEA screenshots if they are clear and explainable.

Design logic

The important part is not just showing that a model exists. The page should explain why the design was made a certain way: constraints, load paths, interfaces, material choices, manufacturing method, and what changed between design versions. 

// what each CAD entry should explain
problem: "what needed to be designed"
constraints: "space, load, cost, manufacturing, user interaction"
decision: "why this design was chosen"
result: "what the model/prototype proved or improved"

Future image layout

Later, CAD images should be placed directly beside the relevant explanation: assembly near assembly discussion, FEA near loading discussion, prototype photos near iteration discussion.

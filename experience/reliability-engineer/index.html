Reliability Engineer — Golden Aluminum

Reliability engineering role focused on manufacturing systems, downtime reduction, preventative maintenance support, troubleshooting, and process improvement.

Role summary

Worked in an aluminum manufacturing environment on reliability improvement, downtime reduction, preventative maintenance support, and recurring equipment issues. A large part of the role involved working through messy problems where documentation was incomplete and the answer was not immediately obvious.

Work performed

  • Supported troubleshooting of recurring manufacturing equipment issues.
  • Worked on reliability and downtime reduction efforts.
  • Supported preventative maintenance improvement.
  • Worked with real production constraints and maintenance communication problems.

Green Belt work

Completed Lean Six Sigma Green Belt work connected to preventative maintenance completion. The main output was a mill monitoring system concept that improved visibility of line status and high-priority maintenance work.

This is included here because it came from professional reliability work, not as a separate standalone project page.
